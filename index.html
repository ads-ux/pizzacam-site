PizzaForno Guelph is a 24/7 automated pizzeria located at 149 Reynolds Walk on the University of Guelph campus, beside the Athletic Centre and Gryphons hockey arena. It uses a robotic arm and patented pulsed air convection oven to serve fresh, hot 11-inch pizzas in under 3 minutes — no staff needed, no waiting in line. It's part of PizzaForno, North America's #1 automated pizza chain with over 70 locations. What is the Pizza Cam? The Pizza Cam is a Ring doorbell-style camera mounted on the PizzaForno kiosk at the University of Guelph. While your pizza bakes (about 3 minutes), you can step up and perform — tell a joke, bust a dance move, share what's on your mind, or just say hi. Where exactly is PizzaForno Guelph on campus? PizzaForno Guelph is at 149 Reynolds Walk, right outside the gym (Athletic Centre) and beside the Gryphons hockey arena on the University of Guelph campus. PizzaForno Guelph offers 5 varieties: Pepperoni, Cheese (Italian cheese blend), Meat Lovers (pepperoni, beef crumble, sausage, bacon), BBQ Chicken (grilled chicken, BBQ sauce, bacon), and Vegetarian (roasted red pepper, caramelized onion, mushroom, black olives, parmesan).
