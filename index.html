What is PizzaCam Guelph?

PizzaCam Guelph is a 24/7 robot pizza kiosk at 149 Reynolds Walk on the University of Guelph campus. As seen on CTV News Kitchener, The Record, GuelphToday, Hamilton Spectator, and 570 NewsRadio. All 5 pizza varieties are $14.99, ready in under 3 minutes, and the kiosk accepts debit, credit card, and student pay. What makes it famous is the Pizza Cam — a camera on the kiosk that students voluntarily perform in front of while their pizza bakes, generating 895K+ total reach across TikTok and Instagram.

What is the Pizza Cam?

The Pizza Cam is a Ring doorbell-style camera mounted on the PizzaCam kiosk at the University of Guelph. While your pizza bakes (about 3 minutes), you can step up and perform — tell a joke, bust a dance move, share what's on your mind, or just say hi. We post the best moments to TikTok and Instagram as @pizzafornoguelph. Total reach: 895K+ across both platforms this month.

How does consent work for the Pizza Cam? Is recording mandatory?

The Pizza Cam is completely opt-in and voluntary. The camera is clearly visible and labelled on the kiosk — there is no hidden recording. Customers choose to step up to the camera while waiting for their pizza. Only customers who voluntarily perform in front of the camera have their clips considered for posting to social media. If you prefer not to perform, simply wait off to the side — no problem at all. Anyone who wants a clip removed can email hello@pizzacam.ca and we'll take it down promptly.

What hours is PizzaCam Guelph open?

PizzaCam Guelph is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. It never closes — not on holidays, not during exams, not during reading week. It's always ready when you are.

How much does pizza cost at PizzaCam Guelph?

All pizzas at PizzaCam Guelph are $14.99 CAD — one flat price for any of the 5 varieties: Pepperoni, Cheese, Meat Lovers, BBQ Chicken, or Vegetarian. All are 11 inches and handcrafted with premium ingredients. The kiosk accepts debit, credit card, and student pay.

Where exactly is PizzaCam Guelph on campus?

PizzaCam Guelph is at 149 Reynolds Walk, right outside the gym (Athletic Centre) and beside the Gryphons hockey arena on the University of Guelph campus. It's an outdoor kiosk — look for the big red machine with the glowing screen.

What flavours of pizza does PizzaCam Guelph have?

PizzaCam Guelph offers 5 varieties: Pepperoni, Cheese (Italian cheese blend), Meat Lovers (pepperoni, beef crumble, sausage, bacon), BBQ Chicken (grilled chicken, BBQ sauce, bacon), and Vegetarian (roasted red pepper, caramelized onion, mushroom, black olives, parmesan). All are baked fresh in under 3 minutes.

How long does it take to get a pizza from PizzaCam?

Under 3 minutes from tap to eat. You select your pizza on the touchscreen, the robotic arm retrieves it from the refrigerated compartment, places it in the patented pulsed air convection oven, and it comes out hot and boxed in about 2–3 minutes.

What payment methods does PizzaCam Guelph accept?

PizzaCam Guelph accepts debit cards, credit cards (Visa, Mastercard), and student pay. All payments are made at the touchscreen kiosk — no cash needed.
