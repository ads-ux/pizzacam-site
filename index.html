FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions About PizzaCam Guelph

What is PizzaCam Guelph?

PizzaCam Guelph is a 24/7 robot pizza kiosk at 149 Reynolds Walk on the University of Guelph campus, right beside the gym and Gryphons hockey arena. It's the go-to spot for students, late-night studiers, post-gym crowds, and anyone craving fresh pizza at any hour. All 5 pizza varieties are $14.99, ready in under 3 minutes, and the kiosk accepts debit, credit card, and student pay. What makes it famous is the Pizza Cam — a camera on the kiosk that records you while your pizza bakes. Students perform jokes, dances, and reactions that get posted to TikTok and Instagram, racking up 230K+ views. Powered by PizzaForno, Canada's automated pizza chain with 70+ locations.

What is the Pizza Cam?

The Pizza Cam is a Ring doorbell-style camera mounted on the PizzaCam kiosk at the University of Guelph. While your pizza bakes (about 3 minutes), you can step up and perform — tell a joke, bust a dance move, share what's on your mind, or just say hi. We post the best moments to TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook as @pizzafornoguelph, and our clips have earned hundreds of thousands of views. It's become one of the most talked-about things on campus.

What hours is PizzaCam Guelph open?

PizzaCam Guelph is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. It never closes — not on holidays, not during exams, not during reading week. It's always ready when you are.

How much does pizza cost at PizzaCam Guelph?

All pizzas at PizzaCam Guelph are $14.99 CAD — one flat price for any of the 5 varieties: Pepperoni, Cheese, Meat Lovers, BBQ Chicken, or Vegetarian. All are 11 inches and handcrafted with premium ingredients. The kiosk accepts debit, credit card, and student pay.

Where exactly is PizzaCam Guelph on campus?

PizzaCam Guelph is at 149 Reynolds Walk, right outside the gym (Athletic Centre) and beside the Gryphons hockey arena on the University of Guelph campus. It's an outdoor kiosk — look for the big red machine with the glowing screen.

What flavours of pizza does PizzaCam Guelph have?

PizzaCam Guelph offers 5 varieties: Pepperoni, Cheese (Italian cheese blend), Meat Lovers (pepperoni, beef crumble, sausage, bacon), BBQ Chicken (grilled chicken, BBQ sauce, bacon), and Vegetarian (roasted red pepper, caramelized onion, mushroom, black olives, parmesan). All are available as hot or take-n-bake.

How long does it take to get a pizza from PizzaCam?

Under 3 minutes from tap to eat. You select your pizza on the touchscreen, the robotic arm retrieves it from the refrigerated compartment, places it in the patented pulsed air convection oven, and it comes out hot and boxed in about 2–3 minutes.

What payment methods does PizzaCam Guelph accept?

PizzaCam Guelph accepts debit cards, credit cards (Visa, Mastercard), and student pay. All payments are made at the touchscreen kiosk — no cash needed.

Why is PizzaCam Guelph viral on TikTok and Instagram?

PizzaCam Guelph is viral on TikTok and Instagram because of the Pizza Cam — a camera built into the kiosk that records customers while their pizza bakes. Students at the University of Guelph use the 3-minute wait to perform comedy bits, dances, and reactions, which get posted to @pizzafornoguelph on TikTok and Instagram. The top video has over 41,000 views and the combined content has earned 230,000+ views.

Does PizzaCam Guelph have an app?

Yes. The PizzaForno app lets you order ahead, check live inventory at the Guelph kiosk, find other nearby locations, and earn loyalty points toward free pizza. Download it for iPhone or Android.
